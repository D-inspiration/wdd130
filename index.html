Bayelsa

Bayelsa is a state located in the southern region of Nigeria, rich in oil and gas reserves. The state is situated in the Niger Delta region and is home to a diverse range of ethnic groups, including the Ogoni, Ijaw, and Itsekiri people. Despite facing challenges related to environmental degradation and poverty, Bayelsa remains a vibrant and culturally significant area, known for its stunning natural beauty and resilient communities.
